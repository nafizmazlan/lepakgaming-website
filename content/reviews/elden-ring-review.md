## Introduction

Shadow of the Erdtree is not just an expansion—it's a full-fledged adventure that could easily stand on its own. FromSoftware has outdone themselves once again.

## Gameplay

The combat mechanics feel even more refined than the base game. New weapon types add fresh strategies, and the boss fights are some of the most challenging yet fair encounters I've experienced.

### New Features

- Over 10 new boss fights
- 8 new weapon categories
- Expanded map larger than Limgrave
- New magic system

## Graphics and Performance

Running on my PC with an RTX 4070, the game maintains a solid 60fps at 1440p on high settings. The new areas are visually stunning, with improved lighting and particle effects.

## Verdict

**Score: 9.5/10**

Shadow of the Erdtree is a masterclass in expansion design. If you loved Elden Ring, this is an absolute must-play.

### Pros
- Incredible boss design
- Massive new area to explore
- Refined combat mechanics
- Great performance

### Cons
- Extremely difficult for newcomers
- Some reused assets from base game
