Ubisoft has officially confirmed that a remake of **Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag** is in active development under the title **Resynced**. The publisher acknowledged the project after months of leaks and job listings hinted at a return to the fan-favorite Caribbean setting.

## Key points

- **Project name**: Assassin's Creed Black Flag — Resynced.  
- **Status**: Early development; Ubisoft has not shared a launch window or target platforms yet.  
- **Studios involved**: Led by Ubisoft Singapore with support from other Assassin's Creed teams.  
- **Why it matters**: Black Flag's naval combat and open-sea exploration remain series highlights, and a modern remake signals Ubisoft's push to refresh classic entries while new mainline titles are in the works.

## What to watch next

Ubisoft says more details—platforms, release timing, and gameplay upgrades—will follow in a future showcase. For now, the confirmation ends speculation and puts the beloved pirate adventure back on the official roadmap.
