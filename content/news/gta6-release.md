Rockstar Games has officially announced the release window for Grand Theft Auto 6 during their latest earnings call. The game is set to launch in Fall 2026 for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X/S, with a PC version to follow.

## Key Announcements

- **Release Window**: Fall 2026
- **Platforms**: PS5 and Xbox Series X/S at launch
- **PC Version**: Coming 6-12 months after console release
- **Setting**: Vice City and surrounding areas

## What This Means

This marks the first official confirmation from Rockstar after years of speculation. The gaming community has been waiting over a decade for this announcement.
