## Major Roster Leak

A retail listing from Bulgarian website Ozone has accidentally revealed two major characters coming to Marvel Tokon: Fighting Souls. According to the leak, both Magneto and Scarlet Witch will be playable fighters when the game launches later this year.

This father-daughter duo represents some of Marvel's most iconic Mutants, making their potential inclusion a huge deal for fans of the franchise. Magneto, in particular, has long been a fan-favorite in fighting games, with memorable appearances in the Marvel vs. Capcom series.

## Following the X-Men Reveal

The leak comes just weeks after PlayStation's State of Play event, where developer Arc System Works officially revealed several X-Men characters for the game. The initial lineup included Storm, Wolverine, Magik, and Danger as part of the "Unbreakable X-Men" team.

With Magneto and Scarlet Witch now potentially joining this roster, Marvel Tokon appears to be building a strong Mutant presence at launch.

## New Stages Revealed

The same retail listing also mentioned three new fighting arenas:

- **Wakanda** - The technologically advanced African nation
- **Sanctum Sanctorum** - Doctor Strange's mystical headquarters  
- **Hellfire Gala** - The X-Men's exclusive high-society event

These locations represent diverse corners of the Marvel universe and suggest the game will feature a wide variety of iconic settings beyond just the X-Men franchise.

## How Reliable Is This Leak?

The leak appears credible for several reasons. First, it came directly from an official retail partner listing the game for pre-order. Second, the specific details about characters and stages were quickly edited out of the product description after the information went public, suggesting someone realized the mistake.

While this points toward the leak being legitimate, it's worth noting that nothing is confirmed until Arc System Works or PlayStation makes an official announcement. Retail listings have been wrong before, though the quick removal of information does lend credibility to this particular leak.

## What We Know About Marvel Tokon

Marvel Tokon: Fighting Souls is being developed by Arc System Works, the studio behind acclaimed fighting games like Guilty Gear and Dragon Ball FighterZ. The game promises to bring Marvel's roster of heroes and villains to life with Arc System's signature anime-inspired art style and fast-paced combat.

The game is currently scheduled to release on August 6th, 2026 for PlayStation 5 and PC. More character reveals are expected in the coming months leading up to launch.

## Community Reaction

Fighting game fans have expressed excitement about the potential addition of Magneto, who was considered one of the strongest and most entertaining characters in previous Marvel fighting games. His mastery of magnetism provides unique gameplay opportunities that have made him a competitive favorite.

Scarlet Witch, while less common in fighting games, brings powerful reality-warping abilities that could translate into interesting mechanics in Arc System's hands.
